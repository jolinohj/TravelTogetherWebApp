WELCOME!

Planning a trip can be time-consuming and challenging, particularly when trying to accommodate everyone's preferences.

TourGether - a word pun combining "Tour" and "Together", is a web app designed to help a group come to a consensus on travel destinations. I hope that with the help of this web app, your travel expereiences (in a group setting) will be more harmonious and enjoyable.

Let's get started~

There are just 3 steps to follow. Refer to the 3 tiles below on what they are then hit the "START" button at the bottom of the page to try it out!

Step 1: Answer these questions:

  1. Location - where are you going?
  2. Duration - for how long?
  3. Group size - how many people?

Step 2: Rank'em!

Each member will rank the places/activities he/she wants to go.do.

Step 3: Populate your itinerary!

Based on what each member has selected, AI will generat the collective results and you can populate your itinerary
