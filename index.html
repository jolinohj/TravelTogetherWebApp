About this website

Planning a trip can be time-consuming and challenging, particularly when trying to accommodate everyone's preferences.

Travel Together is a web app designed to help a group come to a consensus on travel destinations. Hopefully this app will help create a more enjoyable, efficient, and harmonious group travel experience for everyone!
